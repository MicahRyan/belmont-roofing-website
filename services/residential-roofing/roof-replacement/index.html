Replacing your roof: Simpler than you think

A roof is a big deal – and it can be scary to think about replacing it. But we’ve been making it easier for 30 years with our simple process that keeps your peace of mind at the core.

Request a Free Estimate

There are several easy ways to get in touch with us to request a free estimate. The easiest way is to request one here on our website. If that's not for you, just give us a ring at (682) 233-5832. We’ll come out as soon as we can to give you the estimate. If you would like to meet us at your house we can easily arrange that. It often works out well for us to come measure your house when we have time, and then schedule a meeting with you after we have the estimate all put together.

Consider Your Options

We’ll present you with several different high-quality shingle options and help you decide which one would be the best option for you. All of our shingles come with limited manufacturers warranties, but we are certified to provide their highest full-coverage warranties too. Every home is different, so we may offer you other upgrade options if we think they would be valuable to you. But our practice has always been to never try to sell you anything that doesn’t add real value.

Trust It Will Be Done Right

We think long term. This might not seem like a big deal, but almost all poor craftsmanship is due to short term thinking (or inexperience). When you hire us to roof your house you can be assured that we are not going to gloss over any problem areas or opt for the “quick fix” instead of a long term solution. We work hard to make sure it’s done affordably AND done well.

Rest Easy with Great Warranties

Along with a beautiful brand-new roof, you’ll also get a five-year workmanship warranty from Belmont Roofing. Our warranty guarantees the quality of the shingle installation so that you can be confident you won’t have a problem down the road. If for some reason there is an issue, we’ll come back and take care of it immediately. On top of our own workmanship warranty, we are also certified to offer the best manufacturers’ warranties on the market.
