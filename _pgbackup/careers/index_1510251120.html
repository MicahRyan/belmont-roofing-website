Repairs Specialist – Grand Island

We’re picking up steam out west, in a big way! And one of the best ways we can serve our customers in the Grand Island area is to offer the same outstanding repairs options as our Omaha and Lincoln offices. We’re looking for someone with roofing experience to join our team to take care of these customers’ needs, so down the road, they know just who to call when it’s time to replace the roof.

OUR IDEAL CANDIDATE:

  • Has experience with roofing (both steep and flat) and the ability to diagnose and repair problem areas including shingling, roof penetration points, and flashing
  • Is a responsible, motivated self-starter who doesn’t require much supervision
  • Has a driver’s license and clean driving record
  • Is reliable and ready to come to work every day — this is a full-time position!
  • Is bilingual (English and Spanish) — not a requirement, but it’s a plus
  • Will live out our core values of  Trust, Longevity, and  Community

WHAT WE OFFER:

  • Compensation commensurate with experience
  • A safe work environment on each and every job
  • Benefits including health and dental insurance, paid time off, and a 401K plan
  • A path to promotion in a rapidly-growing department — hard work is recognized and rewarded!

TO APPLY:
Please fill out an application online, or print one to fill out. Printed applications may be mailed to the attention of Jared Loudy at PO Box 22133, Lincoln, NE 68542, or dropped off at our Grand Island office at 924 Concord Ave. Please call (308) 675-3330 with any questions.
