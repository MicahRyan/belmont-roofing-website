We make certain you’re happy

Belmont Roofing was recently honored with the CertainTeed Customer Track Record Diamond Approval Award. This award was presented to us by CertainTeed Corporation, one of our shingle partners, acknowledging that we exceeded their superior standards in customer service based upon customer survey results. Since this award was attained by only 24 companies in the U.S. and Canada this year, it’s one of the best honors we can receive — and it’s made that much better for us because it’s determined by happy customers, which is the best reward for hard work we can think of.

A few more awards…

We don’t want to brag — we just hope this helps tell our story a little more. Our only goal is to be the roofing contractor you feel comfortable trusting to work on your home.

Local Awards from our Customers

The most gratifying awards are those we receive as a result of our happy customers. We were named to both the Best of Omaha and Best of Lincoln lists for the last two years, awarded based on community voting. We are truly grateful to our customers for giving us the chance to earn these awards! We were also honored with a Greater Omaha Chamber of Commerce Business Excellence Award   — again, something we deeply treasure, because it means we’ve been able to serve our neighbors and friends in the best way we know how.

Angie’s List Super Service Award

We love Angie’s List because it lets homeowners tell the real story about work they’ve had done. We appreciate real, authentic feedback because we’re always trying to improve our service and offer as much value as we can. We even use Angie’s List to find other contractors when we need to have work done on our own homes!

We were recently honored with the 2016 Angie’s List Super Service Award, which requires earning an “A” rating in overall grade, recent grade, and review period grade; staying in good standing with Angie’s List; passing a background check and abiding by Angie’s List operational guidelines. As a company, we’ve earned this award every year since 2011!

Certainteed Shingle Master

The Shingle Master Certification is given to roofing companies that have been in the business for at least five years, proven they have workers compensation insurance along with liability insurance, have all managers and half of all workers pass the Master Shingle Applicators test, and have consistently maintained a satisfaction rating of a four or higher on a five point scale from all customers who purchased the extended warranty from Certainteed.

GAF Installation Excellence Award

The Excellence Award is only given out to outstanding roofers who earn multiple “perfect” roof installations, based on GAF quality inspections after the roof is complete. Belmont Roofing has been the recipient of this award 5 times to date.

GAF Commercial Roofing Master

The Master Commercial Roofing Contractor Certification from GAF says that Belmont has the proper training, craftsmanship, and insurance to install GAF commercial roofing with its No Dollar Limit Warranties. Big roofs are a big deal.

Supporting our heroes at home

From home to the home team
